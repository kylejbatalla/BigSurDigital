Why Work With Me

A custom site beats a DIY platform

Squarespace and Wix are convenient — but a website built by a local developer gives your business advantages those drag-and-drop platforms simply can't match.

Your site isn't squeezed into a template everyone else is using. Every layout, feature, and detail is built around your business — not the limits of a builder's editor.

Hand-coded sites are leaner and more search-friendly than the bloated code platforms generate. That means better Google rankings and more local customers finding you.

No heavy page-builder overhead. Your site loads quickly on phones and slow connections — which keeps visitors around and helps your search ranking too.
